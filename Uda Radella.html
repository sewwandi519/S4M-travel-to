Uda Radella

Located close to Nuwara Eliya is Uda Radella .Situated along the road to Nanu Oya Ella from Nuwara Eliya the Uda Radella view point can be located. This is one of the main view points where Adams Peak can be seen and is not known by many people.

Things To Do

01.Hike to Uda Radella Viewpoint

Embark on a rewarding hike that begins from Single Tree Hill, leading you through verdant tea plantations to the tranquil village of Shanthipura—the highest village in Sri Lanka. Continuing southwest, you'll reach the Uda Radella viewpoint, which offers sweeping vistas of the central highlands, including the majestic Adam’s Peak on clear days. This trek is approximately 4 km from Single Tree Hill to Shanthipura, with an additional stretch to Uda Radella. For safety and a richer experience, consider hiring a local guide.

02.Visit the Historic Radella Cricket Grounds

Cricket enthusiasts can explore the Radella Cricket Grounds, one of the highest-altitude cricket venues in the world, situated at approximately 4,000 feet above sea level. Established in 1856, this ground is home to the Dimbula Athletic & Cricket Club and has hosted numerous notable matches, including those involving international teams. The picturesque setting amidst rolling hills adds to its charm.
