Royal Turf Club

Situated in the heart of the city, this beautiful Race Course lies alluringly amidst blue mountain ranges, was once one of the most fashionable venues in the 1950s giving an air of Ascot in England. Horse races are held during the April season and are a great attraction among the upper middle class. However others too come here to watch the races. Things To Do

01.mmerse Yourself in Colonial Heritage

Established in 1875, the RTC exudes colonial elegance. The historic grandstand and surrounding architecture provide a glimpse into the British-era traditions of Sri Lanka. The venue's ambiance, combined with the cool climate of Nuwara Eliya, offers a unique setting reminiscent of "Little England."

02.Enjoy Fine Dining and Refreshments

During race days, indulge in a variety of culinary delights. The club offers fine dining options and refreshing beverages, allowing you to savor local and international cuisines while enjoying the races. The impeccable service enhances the overall experience, making it a memorable outing.
