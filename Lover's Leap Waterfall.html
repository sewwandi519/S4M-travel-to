Lover's Leap Waterfall

Lovers leap is an impressive water fall which is known for its romantic folklore. To reach this place one has to go through the tea factory and reach the tea manager’s bungalow along a dirt road. Visitors can reach this destination by following the signs that are there and eventually one can reach a rock face and a Shiva shrine. Just beyond the shrine one can see the cascading water fall. Legend has it that lovers have leapt to their deaths from this water fall and hence the name Lovers Leap.

Things To Do

01.Scenic Hike Through Tea Plantations

Embark on a leisurely 1 km hike to the waterfall, passing through the picturesque Pedro Tea Estate. The trail offers panoramic views of Nuwara Eliya and the surrounding countryside. While the path is relatively easy, it can be slippery during rain, so appropriate footwear is recommended.

02. Photography Opportunities

Capture the mesmerizing beauty of Lover’s Leap, especially during early morning or late afternoon when the lighting enhances the misty ambiance. The waterfall, surrounded by verdant landscapes, provides a perfect backdrop for both amateur and professional photographers.
