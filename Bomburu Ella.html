Bomburu Ella

Also known as Perawella Falls and located near the Nuwara Eliya border. This beautiful cascading mass of water is a collection of 10 smaller falls and is situated about 2000 meters above sea level. Can be reached by taking the Welimada â€“ Pussellawa Road and is reached through a rather winding track. Known to be the widest water fall in Sri Lanka.

Things To Do

01.Embark on a Scenic Hike

Reaching Bomburu Ella involves a 3+ kilometer hike through lush forests and agricultural landscapes. The trail offers glimpses of diverse flora and fauna, making the journey as rewarding as the destination. The hike is moderately challenging, so wearing sturdy footwear is advisable.

02. Explore the Sita Eliya Kandapola Forest Reserve

The waterfall is situated within the Sita Eliya Kandapola Forest Reserve, a region rich in biodiversity. Nature enthusiasts can explore the reserve's diverse ecosystems, which are home to various endemic species of flora and fauna.
